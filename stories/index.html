How the XLab solve the traffic jam issue in one Chinese city?
The occupation of one lane may also reduce the traffic capacity of all lanes on the section of road, and even a short time may cause vehicle queues and traffic congestion if not handled properly, and even lead to regional congestion, due to the high traffic density and strong continuity of Chinese urban roads. Look how the XLab fix this problem by data analysis and machine learning algorithms.
How the XLab predict the profit of the local cocoon industry?
One Chinese county is struggling to expend its cocoon industry. In 1990, its cocoon output was only 0.2 ton, before soaring to 2,468 tons in 2017, with income of 22.27 million Chinese Yuan (equivalent 2.62 Pounds sterling). For finding the tendency and predicting the cocoon industry output, the XLab employs the statistic method to provide the scientific solutions to them.
