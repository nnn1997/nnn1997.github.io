This service includes:

  • Data integration and ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) tools: These tools are used to collect data from various sources (such as databases, spreadsheets, and web applications) and transform it into a standardized format that can be analysed.

  • Data warehousing: This is the process of storing data in a centralized location (a data warehouse) that can be accessed and analyzed by BI tools.

  • Reporting and analysis tools: These tools are used to generate reports and dashboards that provide insights into business performance. They typically allow users to explore data in various ways (such as filtering, sorting, and drilling down), and may include features like data visualization, predictive analytics, and natural language processing.

  • Business intelligence platforms: These are software platforms that provide a suite of BI tools and services, including data integration, data warehousing, reporting, and analysis.
